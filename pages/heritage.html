Our Story

A Family of Weavers

"We didn't start as a shop. We started as weavers — and that is still what we are at heart."

ANR Silks & Shirts was founded in 2011 in the heart of Kanchipuram — the silk capital of South India. What began as a small family enterprise supplying local retailers has grown into a trusted name for authentic handloom across Tamil Nadu.

Our looms have been in the family for generations. Every ANR silk is woven with the same care and attention that our forefathers brought to their craft — no shortcuts, no compromises, no synthetics.

The Silk Capital

Why Kanchipuram?

Kanchipuram has been the undisputed capital of silk weaving in India for over 400 years. Its unique combination of artisan skill, local mulberry cultivation, and cultural identity has produced a silk tradition found nowhere else on earth.

The ANR workshop sits in the heart of the weaving district — a stone's throw from where generations of families have plied their looms. We don't just source from Kanchipuram. We are Kanchipuram.
